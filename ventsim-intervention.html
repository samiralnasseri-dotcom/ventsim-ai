Informed Consent
Please read the following information carefully before deciding to participate.

Study Title

Effect of Generative AI-Driven Standardised Patient Simulation on ICU Nurses' Clinical Decision-Making and Technology Acceptance in Oman: A Quasi-Experimental Study.

Purpose of the Study

This study examines whether AI-guided virtual simulation improves clinical decision-making in mechanical ventilation management among ICU nurses. You are being invited to participate in this research study.

What Will Happen

You will complete: (1) a short demographic form, (2) a pre-test knowledge and clinical decision-making assessment, (3) an orientation tutorial, (4) four virtual ICU simulation scenarios with AI guidance (~40 minutes), (5) a post-test, and (6) a technology acceptance questionnaire. The entire session takes approximately 90 minutes.

The AI Chatbot

During simulation, an AI clinical mentor will ask you Socratic questions to guide your thinking. It will never give you direct answers — it is designed to stimulate your reasoning, not replace it.

Risks and Benefits

There are no foreseeable risks to participation. You may benefit from an enhanced learning experience and contribute to evidence-based nursing education in Oman.

Confidentiality

All data is anonymised. Your participant number (not your name) will be used throughout. Data will be stored securely and used for research purposes only.

Voluntary Participation

Participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without consequence. Your decision will not affect your employment or any other aspect of your work.

Ethics Approval

This study has been approved by the Universiti Malaya Research Ethics Committee (UMREC) and the Ministry of Health Oman.

ORIENTATION
STEP 5 OF 8
Platform Orientation
Before the simulation begins, please familiarise yourself with the interface. This takes approximately 2 minutes.
📺
Ventilator Waveforms
You will see live animated waveforms for Flow, Pressure, and Volume. These change in real time based on patient status. Select the waveform tab to switch between views. Abnormal patterns are key clinical cues.
❤️
Vital Signs Monitor
A live vitals panel shows SpO₂, HR, RR, BP, ETCO₂, and FiO₂. Values highlighted in red indicate alarm ranges — assess these first.
⚡
Clinical Decisions
At each decision point you will choose from 3–4 options. Think carefully — your choice affects the patient's condition and your score. You cannot change a decision once made.
🤖
AI Clinical Mentor
The AI will open each scenario with a question to orient your thinking, then remain available throughout. It will ask you questions — not give answers. Type your thoughts freely. The AI uses the Socratic method to guide you toward insight. You are encouraged to engage with it actively.
📋
Scenario Flow
There are 4 scenarios. Each begins with a patient handover, then unfolds over several decision points. A brief debrief appears after each scenario before you proceed to the next.
💾
Auto-Save
Your progress saves automatically every 2 minutes. If you are interrupted, you can resume from where you left off using your participant number.
⏱ Total simulation time: approximately 40 minutes
🎯 Goal: Make safe, evidence-based clinical decisions for each patient.
🤝 Important: Work independently. Do not consult others during the session.
